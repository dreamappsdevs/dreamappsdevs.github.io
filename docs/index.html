Our Global Privacy Commitment

At Dream Apps Labs, we build tools that empower your daily life without monitoring it. We strictly adhere to a **Zero-Cloud, Offline-First** philosophy. We do not maintain server-side databases, track your accounts, or share your usage logs. Every byte of information generated within our apps is sandboxed securely on your device.

Our business is utility, not advertising. We will never sell, lease, or monetize user data.
